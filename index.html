You leave the comforts of your cabin in the dark woods in search of glory and wealth. At a local inn you hear a rumor of the wizards library. It is said that there is treasure there for the taking! If...you can get past the creatures that guard it.

HA! You survived! Good for you. In the headmaster's chamber of the wizards library you rummage through the desk and discover a note. The note reads: 'I no longer can wield this power. Soon enough the Dark Lord will take back that what was given...Damn it all! I should have known not to meddle in powers beyond me'--Headmaster Lorgrim III. on the back of the note you see a map. the title reads 'Maze of Governance'. Call me a fool, but I think its worth a look.

As you travel the maze you begin to miss your home. How long has it been? days? weeks? The fog of the maze blurred the lines of reality; and in turn, time itself. as you get to the center of the maze the fog starts to lift, you gain your bearings and notice a shimmering light. You follow it to a room filled to the brim with treasure. Shocked and excited you go to scoop some loot! As your hand grasps the coins the piles of gold suddenly turn to bones. A beast bursts out of the pile and you prepare for combat!

The creature is Slain! Gasping for air you hear a voice: "You fool, to meddle in the arts of which you do not understand..". Confused (mainly because you just wanted some treasure and not power) the voice continues:
"I was once an Adventurer like you...until I took an arrow to the knee...(cough)..you must continue through the Thorns of Curation..then to the mountain...be wary....The dark...Lor...".
----------------
The voice fades. You reach for the note you previously found in the Wizard's Library to find it missing. Perhaps it was the voice of the headmaster?...No...no cant be. Besides, ghosts are'nt real...right?

You camped at the forest edge. Sleep did not come to you that night. Looking beyond the fire into the black abyss of the woods a gripping fear reaches you. Not a sound is made, nor a glimmer of light. In fact peering into the darkness it seems to be endless. A dark void with no end, a black abyss pulling you inward. You clutch the ground as if at any moment it would swallow you whole...
---------------
You recall this feeling as you venture inwards. The sun is high, yet no light makes it to this cursed forest floor...you feel eyes on you...you sense the bloodlust in the air...you pause...the hair stands on the back of your neck..silence...then...
(snap)

You deal the final blow to the creature and slump down in the darkness. you wipe the blood from your blade when a chill runs down your spine. Around you you feel the presence of many beings..human?..no...beast?...cant be...You reach down to the corpse of the enemy you have just slain and surgically remove the head. You let out a bestial roar and hurl the head into the darkness. Immediately the scampering of many creatures fade off into the distance.
--------------
As you exit the forest on the north side you make a note in your journal stating: "sometimes, you gotta bluff"
